### The Splinterings

The northeastern archipelago of tribal clans. Fierce independence, blood feuds, and raiding culture.

#### Conflict Generator

| d6  | Source                | Stakes             |
| --- | --------------------- | ------------------ |
| 1   | Blood debt            | Honor/status       |
| 2   | Resource dispute      | Survival/wealth    |
| 3   | Succession crisis     | Power              |
| 4   | Broken oath           | Trust/alliance     |
| 5   | Territorial claim     | Land/sovereignty   |
| 6   | Theft/raid gone wrong | Property/vengeance |

#### Conflict Complication

| d6  | Complication                                            |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Third clan benefits from conflict continuing            |
| 2   | The "wronged" party was actually the original aggressor |
| 3   | Marriage alliance on the table, one party stalling      |
| 4   | Someone wants to escalate to draw in allies             |
| 5   | Key evidence everyone relies on is forged               |
| 6   | Supernatural element (omen, curse, prophecy) involved   |

### The Navel

The wealthy center of the known world. Imperial power, the Wayfarer's Guild, and endless intrigue.

#### Districts

| d10 | District/Area                                              |
| --- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Crystal Isle (imperial palace, high nobility)              |
| 2   | Guild Quarter (Wayfarer's Guild HQ, associated businesses) |
| 3   | Foreign Quarter (traders from Splinterings, Old Emporium)  |
| 4   | Temple District                                            |
| 5   | The Docks (warehouses, sailors, smugglers)                 |
| 6   | Scholars' Row (bookshops, tutors, minor academics)         |
| 7   | The Warrens (poor, dense, criminal)                        |
| 8   | Merchant Heights (wealthy traders, new money)              |
| 9   | Old City (ancient buildings, old families, secrets)        |
| 10  | The Ferries (connects islands, transient population)       |

| d6  | Current Situation                                      |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Festival or public event (cover for other activities)  |

| 3   | Construction/renovation uncovering something           |
| 4   | Power vacuum (someone important died/fled/arrested)    |
| 5   | Foreigners arriving in unusual numbers                 |
| 6   | Plague, fire, or other disaster (recent or threatened) |
