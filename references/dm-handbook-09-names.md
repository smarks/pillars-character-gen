**Fuller Names:** Add a syllable from Column C, or combine two results.

## Pronunciation Guide

**Hard Rules for Pillars Names:**
- "th" is always soft (as in "think," not "the")
- "ae" sounds like "ay" (Aethon = "AY-thon")
- "ei" sounds like "eye" (Eidrik = "EYE-drik")
- Stress falls on the first syllable unless the name ends in "-ia" or "-ius"
- Double consonants are pronounced distinctly (Kellan = "KEL-lan")
